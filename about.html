Why TarkaScience Exists

TarkaScience was created to make science understandable, accessible and exciting for everyone.

Many scientific topics — especially cosmology, physics and quantum mechanics — feel complex and difficult to understand. TarkaScience aims to break those barriers by explaining these ideas in a simple, visual and storytelling-driven way.

The goal is not just to share facts, but to spark curiosity and inspire people to explore the universe and science more deeply.

Mission

To explain complex scientific ideas in a simple and engaging way so anyone can understand them.

Every TarkaScience video is based on real research and verified scientific sources, ensuring accuracy while keeping the explanations easy to follow.

Vision

  • • Make science accessible to everyone
  • • Inspire curiosity about the universe
  • • Promote free learning and knowledge sharing
  • • Build a global science learning community
